<p><ul><li>Coarse Grind</li><li>Fine Grind</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The wafer grinding wheel market features two primary types: coarse grind and fine grind wheels. Coarse grind wheels are designed for rapid material removal, effectively shaping and thinning wafers during the initial grinding process. These wheels typically have a larger grit size, allowing for efficient grinding of hard materials. In contrast, fine grind wheels provide a smoother finish and greater precision, ideal for final polishing and achieving optimal surface quality on the wafers. Both types cater to specific applications in semiconductor manufacturing.</p></p>
