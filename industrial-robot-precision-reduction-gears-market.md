<p><p>The Industrial Robot Precision Reduction Gears market includes two primary types: RV precision reduction gears and harmonic precision reduction gears. RV gears are known for their high torque capacity and compact design, making them ideal for heavy-duty applications and multi-axis robots. In contrast, harmonic gears utilize a unique mechanism that allows for large gear reductions in a smaller footprint, offering high precision and flexibility. Both types enhance robotic performance through improved accuracy and efficiency, catering to various industrial automation needs.</p></p>
