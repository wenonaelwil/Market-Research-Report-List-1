<p><p>The at-home hair dye market encompasses three main types: semi-permanent, permanent, and temporary hair dyes. Semi-permanent hair dye deposits color without significantly altering the hair's structure, typically lasting several weeks. Permanent hair dye, on the other hand, penetrates the hair shaft for long-lasting color, often requiring touch-ups as roots grow. Temporary hair dye offers a short-lived color option, washing out after a few shampoos, making it ideal for experimentation or special occasions. Each type caters to diverse consumer preferences and needs.</p></p>
