<p><p>Chitosan edible packaging films are categorized into three crystal forms: α, β, and γ. The α crystal form is characterized by higher solubility and flexibility, making it suitable for a variety of food applications. The β form offers improved mechanical strength and barrier properties, ideal for prolonged shelf life. The γ form combines both solubility and strength, providing a versatile option for coatings and wraps. Each form has distinct properties, catering to different packaging needs in the food industry.</p></p>
