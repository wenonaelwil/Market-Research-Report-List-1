<p><ul><li>Passive Car Alarm</li><li>Active Car Alarm</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive alarms market comprises two main types: passive and active car alarms. Passive car alarms automatically engage when the vehicle is locked, providing continuous monitoring without user intervention. They typically include features like immobilizers and alarm sirens. In contrast, active car alarms require manual activation by the user, often via a remote control or smartphone app. This type allows for more user control, offering functions like GPS tracking and customizable alerts, enhancing vehicle security based on user preference.</p></p>
